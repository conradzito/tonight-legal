Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Tonight is operated by Human Hero (CVR: 42585971), owned by Conrad Elliot Brandt Elmholt, Copenhagen, Denmark. Questions about this policy: kontakt@tonight-app.dk

1. Who We Are

Tonight ("the App") is a dating application that helps people meet nearby. The App is developed and operated by Human Hero, a sole proprietorship registered in Denmark (CVR: 42585971), owned by Conrad Elliot Brandt Elmholt.

As a Danish business, we are subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Danish Data Protection Act (Databeskyttelsesloven). We act as the data controller for all personal data collected through the App.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data: Email address, password (stored encrypted), name, age, gender, and bio.
  • Profile photos: Photos you upload to your profile, stored securely in our cloud storage.
  • Location data: Your approximate GPS location, used to calculate and display distance to other users. We do not store a continuous location history — only your most recently updated position.
  • Usage data: Swipe actions (likes and passes), matches, and chat messages.
  • Device data: Basic device information collected automatically by our infrastructure provider (Supabase).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data solely to operate the App and provide you with its features:

  • To show you potential matches based on your preferences and location
  • To enable matching, messaging, and the Wall feature between users
  • To enforce our Community Guidelines and keep the platform safe
  • To respond to support requests and reports of abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data on the following legal bases under GDPR Article 6:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to provide the App's core features.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)): Safety, fraud prevention, and moderation of reported content.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Location data and optional profile information, which you can withdraw at any time.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Where required by Danish or EU law.

5. Location Data

Tonight uses your device's GPS to show other users approximately how far away you are. Location is only collected when you have the App open and have granted permission. We store only your most recently known position — we do not build a history of your movements.

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings. If you do, distance will not be shown on your profile.

6. Photos

Photos you upload are stored in secure cloud storage provided by Supabase (hosted in the EU). Photos are visible to other users of the App. You can delete your photos at any time from your Profile screen. When you delete your account, all photos are permanently deleted.

7. Chat Messages

Messages sent between matched users are stored in our database to enable the chat feature. Messages are only visible to the two users in the conversation. We do not read private messages except where required to investigate a safety report.

8. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following third-party service providers, who process data on our behalf:

  • Supabase Inc. — database, authentication, file storage, and real-time features. Data is stored in EU-based servers. Supabase is GDPR compliant.
  • Expo (Expo Technology Inc.) — mobile app build and update infrastructure.

We do not share your data with any other third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all of your personal data — including your profile, photos, matches, and messages — is permanently deleted within 30 days.

10. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can correct inaccurate data directly in the App or by contacting us.
  • Right to erasure: You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the Profile screen in the App.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent: You can withdraw consent (e.g. location) at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kontakt@tonight-app.dk. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Danish Data Protection Authority (Datatilsynet) at www.datatilsynet.dk.

11. Children

Tonight is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will delete their account immediately. If you believe a minor is using the App, please contact us at kontakt@tonight-app.dk.

12. Security

We take data security seriously. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Access to the database is restricted and protected. However, no system is completely secure — if you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify users within the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
